Our story

A neighbourhood
escape, framed by palms.

Tucked into Oasis Bhawan, our café was built around a simple idea — that good coffee deserves time, and the people drinking it deserve a quiet place to meet it. Warm wood, soft light, and palms framing every corner.

Our beans are single‑estate, 100% Arabica, sourced from the misty hills of Karnataka. Slow‑grown, hand‑picked, small‑batch roasted. What you taste is rich aroma, full body, with notes of cocoa, roasted almond and a hint of pepper.
